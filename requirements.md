# actions-runner — Requirements

## Goals
Verify the end-to-end functionality of the fleet infrastructure using the `actions-runner` repository.

## Requirements
1. Validate member registration and heartbeat.
2. Execute a sequence of shell commands and prompts.
3. Verify file transfer (send/receive) functionality.
4. Test permission enforcement (allow/deny).
5. Confirm Beads task tracking works across members.

## Success Criteria
- All 5 requirement points are verified and documented.
- The `actions-runner` member remains healthy throughout the test.
- The Beads DB correctly reflects the task lifecycle.
